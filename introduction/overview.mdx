## What are Voice Agents?

Voice agents are AI-powered systems that can **listen, understand, and respond to human speech in real time**.

They combine several technologies to create natural conversations between humans and machines.

Modern voice agents typically use:

- 🎙 **Speech-to-Text (STT)** to convert speech into text  
- 🧠 **Large Language Models (LLMs)** to understand and generate responses  
- 🛠 **Tools or APIs** to perform actions  
- 🔊 **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** to convert responses back into audio  

These components work together to create a seamless **voice interaction pipeline**.

---

## Why Voice Agents?

Voice interfaces are becoming increasingly common in applications such as:

- Customer support assistants  
- Smart home devices  
- AI tutors and learning assistants  
- Appointment booking systems  
- Voice-powered chatbots  

Unlike traditional chatbots, voice agents provide a **more natural and accessible user experience**.
